SMB3 Card Game Tracker 1.2
by Macobra52

Instructions:

Open index.html

Left click on a card to display the menu, allowing you to select a card to assign to it. If you make a mistake simply left click the card again to assign a different card, or right click to reset it to default.

Right click will hide the menu if it is displayed.

Reset - resets all cards to default.
Open new window - Opens another N card tracker, automatically adjusting the window size accordingly.

Tips:

-Enable 'Background Input' in your preferred emulator to more efficiently track while you play.
-Use the Open new window button to automatically convert the tracker to a small adjustable window.

Happy Matching! :-)
